About Me

I am a research-driven stem cell biologist focusing on the interface of pluripotent stem cells and neuroimmunology. My academic journey bridges developmental biology, immunotherapy, and functional modeling of human microglia for translational applications in neurodegenerative diseases.

Research Focus

  • Modeling neuroinflammation and aging-related decline using stem cell-derived immune cells
  • Single-cell analysis and transcriptomic profiling of immune cell identity
  • GMP-compliant culture systems for scalable cell therapy platforms
